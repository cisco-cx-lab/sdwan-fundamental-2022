CX SD-WAN Bootcamp Lab Guide

Introduction

Welcome to CX SD-WAN bootcamp. This lab covers SD-WAN base and advanced knowledge including configuring feature template/device template, local routing policy, local data policy, central routing policy, control policy, app-aware routing policy and some common use cases like DIA, QoS etc.

Unlike other bootcamp's lab guide, this lab guide doesn't include detail step by step instructions. Instead we give you the objective and intent for each task, you need to do your research and complete the task.

Upon completion of the task, you need upload screenshots or configuration files to show the results to your instructor.

2. Lab Topology

The lab is built with (NFV) network function virtualization. There is no physical appliance in the lab. To view the topology for each SDWAN site, login POC tool console and click the site name from the Topology View on the left.
